CoreCode Privacy Policy 1.1


CoreCode believes strongly in privacy rights and therefore does not collect any data from its customers.

Specifically, our apps do not transmit ANY data from our customers to us we do NOT track or collect any kind of information of or about our customers in any way.


The only cases where our apps may contact our servers are:

• apps distributed outside the (Mac) App Store may have a built-in update mechanism (Sparkle) that downloads a XML file from our servers to check whether newer versions are available. No data is transmitted from the app to our server in this case.

• some apps may download and display HTML, TEXT or MOVIE files from our server. No data is transmitted from the app to our server in this case.

• some apps may have a option to use our server to send notification e-mails. The information about the mails to be sent is stored temporarily on the server but only used in case the delivery fails to manually re-send those mails.
